// Why LukiLinux

Designed to be fast.
Not just marketed as it.

Most distros promise speed but ship with a dozen daemons running in the background. LukiLinux actually delivers — you'll notice it from the first boot.

⚡

Boots in seconds

Stripped-down init process means you're at your desktop in under 10 seconds on most hardware. No waiting around.

🧠

Low RAM footprint

Idles at around 300MB of RAM. That's it. Plenty of headroom for what you actually want to run.

📦

Ubuntu compatible

Built on top of Ubuntu, so every .deb package, PPA, and apt command you already know just works.

🔧

No bloat, ever

We don't ship games, office suites, or apps you didn't ask for. Install what you want, nothing more.

🖥️

Runs on old hardware

Got a laptop from 2013 collecting dust? LukiLinux will breathe new life into it without breaking a sweat.

🔒

Stays up to date

Built on Ubuntu LTS, so you get long-term security updates without chasing rolling releases.
